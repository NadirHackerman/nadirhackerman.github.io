About SwissElis

Swiss precision applied
to global trade and advisory.

Founded in Switzerland, SwissElis is an international trading and advisory company serving the chemical, food, biological, and related sectors. We combine commercial agility with technical understanding to support sourcing, distribution, market development, consulting, and long-term business growth.

  • Swiss-based platform with international scope
  • Expertise across raw materials, intermediates, finished products, and equipment
  • Advisory support spanning supply chain, procurement, management, and R&D-related activities
